Inés Morán
Dietitian - Nutritionist

In my consultation I enhance the individualisation of each person, so that together we can achieve the best version of ourselves. Without restrictions, prohibitions, or guilt that hinder a path of learning and habit change. Whatever your goal may be, let's go for it together.

How can I help you?

Clinical nutrition

Through tailored sessions, I will guide you through the fundamental concepts of nutrition and will assist you with:

  • Nutritional education
  • Changing eating habits
  • Vegetarian or vegan diets

Sports nutrition

In competitive sport, nutrition plays a crucial role; I can assist you with:

  • Improving athletic performance
  • Nutritional education in sports
  • Planning nutrition for competitions

Talks, training and content

Need a podcast guest, conference talk, or trainer? I love sharing my expertise in person and online.
